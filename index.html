PICARIELLO DATA CHALLENGE 2022

Welcome to the official page of the Picariello Data Challenge 2022 organized by the University of Naples "Federico II" and sponsored by Eustema s.p.a.

If you want to know more about the challenge press → on your keyboard.

The Challenge

The objective of the challenge is to simulate an industrial Data Science project as you would encounter working as a data scientist for a company or an interdisciplinary research group. The challenge is about predicting the outcome, the duration in number of days and the settlement for around 300,000 lowsuits produced by several tribunals of the "Giudice di Pace" scattered around Italy.
If you want to go streight to the data, look here


At the beginning of the challenge, each group will be provided with training set containing all the input data fields and the values for the target variables and a blind test set for which you will have to make predictions.
